Gender Equality

Achieving gender equality and empowering all women and girls.

Goal Overview

SDG 5 aims to achieve gender equality and empower all women and girls. It seeks to end all forms of discrimination, violence, and harmful practices, as well as ensuring equal participation in leadership roles, access to education, and economic resources.
